Commitments
  1. Adequate Girls hostels and Girls Toilets
  2. Follow the state reservation policy
  3. Building to be disable friendly and also ensure special facilities/equipment for the disabled
  4. Adequate per student provisions for classrooms, library and laboratory facilities
  5. Inclusion of ICT in teaching-learning process and provision of internet access to all students
  6. Implementation of all academic, examination and governance reforms
  7. Aim to achieve a better student teacher ratio (within a time-frame)
  8. Aim to achieve a better Teaching to Non-Teaching Staff (within a time-frame)
  9. All the recurring expenditure (including salaries) and non-recurring expenditure beyond the scope of the approval under the scheme would be met by the State Government.
