Fund Demand Proposal
* Note :

1. One can raise fund demand one at a time ONLY.

2. Unless fund demand proposal is final submitted with eSign(AADHAR based), one can not raise new request.

3. Proposal can be submitted/updated by any state level authorities (SAA/SNO/SPD) but proposal final submission using eSign(AADHAR based) can be done by SAA ONLY.

4. Eligibility criteria for raising fund demand proposal are:
a. There must not be state share shortfall of fund.
b. Central and State Share utilization percentage must be greater than or equal to seventy-five percentage(75%).

5.Central/State Utilised Percentage = (Utilised/Released)*100.

6. Shortfall Calculation Formula:
a. Percentage of Central Share Released = (Central share Released/Central Share Approved)*100. It excludes MMER amount.
b. State Due Share to be Released (excluding MMER) = (State Share Approved * Percentage of Central Share Released)/100.
c. Shortfall(State) = Due Share to be Released-State Share Released.
